# SPEC-005

# KAVEEP Command Center Experience Center

Version: 1.0

Status: Draft

Repository: KAVEEP-COMMAND-CENTER

Classification: Enterprise Architecture

---

# 1. Purpose

The Experience Center is the official user experience management platform of the KAVEEP ecosystem.

Its responsibility is to provide a personalized, adaptive, consistent, and efficient interaction layer between users and the KAVEEP ecosystem.

It transforms complex enterprise operations into intuitive user experiences.

---

# 2. Mission

Deliver a unified experience that minimizes cognitive load while maximizing visibility, productivity, transparency, and trust.

---

# 3. Design Principles

## Principle 1

The user should always know what the system is doing.

---

## Principle 2

Important information should appear before requested information.

---

## Principle 3

The system should adapt to users without changing governance.

---

## Principle 4

Consistency is more important than visual complexity.

---

## Principle 5

Every action should be understandable.

---

## Principle 6

Users remain in control.

---
