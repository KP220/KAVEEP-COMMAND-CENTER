# SPEC-010

# KAVEEP Command Center Audit Center

Version: 1.0

Status: Draft

Repository: KAVEEP-COMMAND-CENTER

Classification: Enterprise Architecture

---

# 1. Purpose

The Audit Center is the official observability, traceability, and forensic platform of the KAVEEP ecosystem.

It provides complete visibility into every significant action, decision, workflow, policy evaluation, consensus session, and security event.

The Audit Center is the permanent memory of operational history.

---

# 2. Mission

Provide a trusted, immutable, searchable, and explainable audit platform that enables accountability, compliance, troubleshooting, governance, and continuous improvement.

---

# 3. Design Principles

## Principle 1

Every significant action shall be recorded.

---

## Principle 2

Audit records are immutable.

---

## Principle 3

---

## Principle 4

---

## Principle 5

---

## Principle 6
