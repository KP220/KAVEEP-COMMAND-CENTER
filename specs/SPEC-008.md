# SPEC-008

# KAVEEP Command Center Knowledge Center

Version: 1.0

Status: Draft

Repository: KAVEEP-COMMAND-CENTER

Classification: Enterprise Architecture

---

# 1. Purpose

The Knowledge Center is the official knowledge governance platform of the KAVEEP ecosystem.

It is responsible for managing, validating, organizing, versioning, searching, protecting, and distributing knowledge across all KAVEEP platforms.

The Knowledge Center does not determine truth.

Truth is established through the KAVEEP Consensus Protocol (KCP).

The Knowledge Center preserves verified knowledge.

---

# 2. Mission

Provide a trusted, transparent, auditable, and continuously evolving knowledge platform that becomes the single source of truth for the KAVEEP ecosystem.
