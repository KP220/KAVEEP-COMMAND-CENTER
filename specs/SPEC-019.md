# KAVEEP Command Center Ecosystem Monitoring

Version: 1.0

Status: Draft

Repository: KAVEEP-COMMAND-CENTER

Classification: Enterprise Architecture

---

# 1. Purpose

The Ecosystem Monitoring Center is the official operational intelligence platform of the KAVEEP ecosystem.

Its responsibility is to continuously observe, analyze, evaluate, and report the health, stability, performance, and governance status of the entire ecosystem.

Monitoring is continuous.

Monitoring is predictive.

Monitoring supports decision making.

---

# 2. Mission

Provide real-time operational intelligence that enables proactive governance instead of reactive troubleshooting.

---

# 3. Design Principles

# 19. Architecture Rules

Monitoring shall remain read-only.

Monitoring shall never directly modify the ecosystem.

Recommendations require Policy validation before execution.

Automation shall never be triggered directly by monitoring without governance.
