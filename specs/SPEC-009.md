Version: 1.0

Status: Draft

Classification: Enterprise Architecture

---

# 1. Purpose

The KCP Center is the official operational interface for the KAVEEP Consensus Protocol (KCP).

It coordinates, supervises, visualizes, records, and governs all consensus activities across the KAVEEP ecosystem.

The KCP Center never determines truth independently.

Truth is established only through the KCP process.

---

# 2. Mission

Provide a transparent, auditable, explainable, and scalable consensus platform capable of validating important decisions using multiple independent reasoning agents and evidence sources.

---

# 3. Design Principles

## Principle 1

No important conclusion shall depend on a single agent.

---

## Principle 2

Consensus is evidence-driven.

---

## Principle 3

Evidence quality is more important than evidence quantity.

---

## Principle 4

Independent reasoning is required.

---

## Principle 5

Insufficient evidence shall result in

UNVERIFIED

Never TRUE.

Never FALSE.

---

## Principle 6

Consensus must remain observable.
